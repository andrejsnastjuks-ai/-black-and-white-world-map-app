## Full description
Black World Map turns your real travels into a personal adventure map. The world starts dark. Every walk, road, trail, and journey reveals the map around you and saves your explored places forever.

Use it for city walks, road trips, hiking, family travel, and long-term travel memories.

Features:
- Reveal the map as you move.
- Record trips with GPS.
- Save your travel history.
- See distance statistics.
- Export GPX routes.
- Keep your data local on your device.

Your world is waiting to be discovered.
