# Monetization Plan

## Recommended model
Freemium + Premium subscription.

## Free version
- Record trips.
- Reveal map locally.
- Basic stats.
- Limited trip history or limited export count.

## Premium version
- Unlimited trip history.
- Cloud sync.
- Family/shared maps.
- Advanced statistics.
- Year recap.
- Photo memories.
- Offline map packs.
- Custom map themes.

## Suggested pricing
- Monthly: 2.99–4.99 EUR.
- Yearly: 19.99–29.99 EUR.
- Lifetime early supporter: 39.99–59.99 EUR.

## Best first premium feature
Cloud sync + family map. This is easy for users to understand and creates strong emotional value.

## Avoid at launch
- Too many ads.
- Paywalling basic GPS recording.
- Complex pricing tiers.
