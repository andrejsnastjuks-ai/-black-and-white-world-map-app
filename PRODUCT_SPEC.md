# Black World Map — Product Specification

## Product vision
A travel app where the world starts hidden. Every walk, road trip, flight transfer, city visit, hike, and family journey reveals the map permanently. The product should feel like a real-life adventure game for travelers.

## Core promise
- The map is dark until the user physically explores.
- Movement reveals roads, trails, towns, and countries.
- Users can collect places, trips, achievements, photos, and personal travel history.

## Target users
1. Travelers who want a beautiful personal map.
2. Families who want to track shared adventures.
3. Hikers and road-trip users.
4. People who like gamified progress, achievements, and exploration.

## MVP features for first paid release
- GPS trip recording.
- Dark world map experience.
- Persistent discovered cells.
- Trip history.
- Distance statistics.
- GPX export.
- Local-first privacy.
- Manual start/stop recording.
- Background tracking with Android foreground notification.
- Clear permission screens explaining location use.

## Version 1.1 features
- Cloud account and sync.
- Google/Apple sign-in.
- Country and city progress.
- Achievements.
- Better offline tile/cache handling.
- Photos attached to trips.

## Version 1.2 features
- Family/shared map.
- Private groups.
- Trip albums.
- Heatmap and year recap.
- Premium subscription.

## Success metrics
- First trip completed.
- Day 7 retention.
- Trips recorded per user.
- Kilometers revealed.
- Premium conversion.
- Crash-free sessions.
